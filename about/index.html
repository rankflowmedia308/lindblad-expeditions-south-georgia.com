About This Site

An independent comparison of Lindblad Expeditions' South Georgia route and the operators running the same itinerary.

This site exists to give travelers researching Lindblad Expeditions' South Georgia, Antarctica & Falklands itinerary a clear, comparative view of the route — its ships, pricing, and the smaller-ship alternatives running similar itineraries in the same season.

What we cover

We focus specifically on the South Georgia expedition cruise market: operators running multi-week itineraries through the Antarctic Peninsula, South Georgia, and the Falkland Islands. Our content draws on publicly available itinerary documents, ship specifications, IAATO and AECO membership records, and verified Google Reviews data for each operator we cover.

Why we started with Lindblad

Lindblad Expeditions, through its National Geographic-branded ships, is one of the most-searched operators on this specific route. We use its itinerary as the baseline for comparison because it's a well-documented, widely booked voyage — not because we have any commercial relationship with the company.

How we make money

This site may include links to operator websites and third-party booking platforms. Some of these links may be affiliate links, meaning we could earn a commission if you book through them — at no additional cost to you. This does not influence our rankings or comparisons. See our Editorial Policy for how we determine ranking order.

Accuracy and updates

Pricing, ship specifications, and itinerary details are sourced from each operator's published materials and verified at the time of writing. Expedition cruise fares and itineraries change seasonally; we recommend confirming final details directly with any operator before booking. Last updated: July 2026.
