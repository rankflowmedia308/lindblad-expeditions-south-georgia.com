Cookie Policy

How this site uses cookies and how to manage your preferences.

What cookies we use

This site uses a minimal set of cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party profiling tools.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to measure anonymous traffic — page views, referral sources, and general geographic region. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable. Google Analytics sets its own cookies; you can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Reviews (Places API)

Review data on the homepage is fetched in real time from the Google Places API. This request originates from your browser and is subject to Google's own privacy policy. No additional cookies are set by this functionality.

How to manage cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to read content on this site. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari

Third-party links

This site contains links to external operator websites and booking platforms. We are not responsible for the cookie or privacy practices of those sites.
